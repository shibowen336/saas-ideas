## Anchor text mix

Keep anchors natural. Do not overuse exact match anchors.

- Brand: `SaaS Idea Validator`
- Brand + topic: `SaaS Idea Validator tool`
- Partial match: `tool to validate a SaaS idea`
- Informational: `SaaS idea validation examples`
- Informational: `how to validate a SaaS idea`

Recommended mix:

- 50% brand or naked URL
- 30% partial match
- 20% exact or near-exact match

## Channel-by-channel action notes

### Product Hunt

- Use the tool page as the launch URL.
- Prepare one strong tagline.
- Prepare at least four gallery assets.
- Post a first-maker comment immediately after launch.
- Link back to the examples page in launch comments only when relevant.

### BetaList

- Keep the product description concise.
- Emphasize the founder use case: validate before building.
- Do not position it as a generic AI idea generator.

### Peerlist Launchpad

- Use a maker-style framing.
- Show a screenshot of the input form and one example report.
- Keep the project description practical.

### Indie Hackers

- Do not lead with "please try my product."
- Lead with the framework, the problem, or what you learned while building.
- Best post angle: why founders need idea validation before MVP work.

### Reddit

- Prefer useful posts over launch posts.
- Best angle for `r/SaaS`: lessons from validating SaaS ideas.
- Best angle for `r/startups`: free SaaS idea validation template.
- Best angle for `r/SideProject`: what the product does and who it helps.
