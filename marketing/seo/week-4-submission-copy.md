## Short description

### 80 to 120 words

SaaS Idea Validator helps founders evaluate startup ideas before they spend weeks building the wrong product. It scores demand, competition pressure, audience clarity, monetization potential, MVP simplicity, and next-step validation actions, then turns those inputs into a structured report with risks, positioning suggestions, and practical next steps.

## Longer product description

SaaS Idea Validator is built for indie hackers, solo founders, and early-stage builders who want to validate a SaaS idea before committing to development. Instead of relying on instinct alone, the tool helps founders stress-test buyer pain, alternatives, monetization, competition, and MVP scope.

After entering the idea and a few details such as the target user, problem, pricing angle, and early go-to-market plan, users get a structured report with scores, risks, wedge recommendations, and next-step validation actions. The goal is not to replace real market research. The goal is to help founders make sharper early decisions before they build.
